Progression Toward Entrustment

In my first semester of DVM3 I have reflected on my progression toward entrustment of key competencies in veterinary education called Entrustable Professional Activities (EPAs). Attached are those reflective pieces. Overall, I feel I have achieved the following entrustment levels for EPAs 1, 2, 4 and 5:

EPA 1: Gather a history, perform an examination, and create a prioritised differential diagnosis list EPA 2: Develop a diagnostic plan and interpret results EPA 4: Recognise a patient requiring urgent or emergent care and initiate evaluation and management EPA 5: Formulate relevant questions and retrieve evidence to advance care
  • History Taking: 4
  • Clinical Examination: 3 (for most species)
  • 2 (horses)
  • DDX list creating 2
 3.5 3 4
These levels are based on a 1-5 scale following on the guidelines laid out by the Association of American Vetrinary Medical Colleges.
